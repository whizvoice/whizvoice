Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2025

What Information We Collect

Voice Data: We use Android speech recognition on your device and do not save your voice data.
Usage Information: We collect basic information about how you use the service, such as when you interact with the assistant and what features you use.
Device Information: We may collect basic information about your device, such as device type and operating system.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our voice assistant service
  • Process your voice commands and provide responses
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our AI capabilities
  • Ensure the security and proper functioning of our service

Data Storage and Security

Sensitive user preferences such as API keys are stored encrypted.

Data Retention

User preferences and chat history are stored in a database. We do have chat deletion on our roadmap but it is not an available feature yet.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data

Third-Party Services

We require you to have your own subscription to third party services to use those integrations.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for minors. We do not knowingly collect personal information from people under the age of 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact Ruth.
